Problem Statement: Locating a Living Space

People moving are facing difficulties in finding the correct location that has all of the businesses and facilities they need to comfortably live within a preferred distance. Our solution should provide an easy way to determine the best place of living, such as an apartment or home, with the most everyday businesses or facilities, like workplaces, schools, grocery stores, and gyms, nearest to them.
